Visualization of Algebraic Structures - Symmetric Groups

Welcome! This project provides interactive visualizations of Cayley graphs of symmetric groups. Each visualization explores different generator sets and subgroup structures. Click the items on the sidebar to explore various Cayley graphs and learn more about the algebraic structures behind them.

We mainly focus on the symmetric group \(S_n\), the group of all permutations on \(n\) elements. Each Cayley graph represents the group structure visually, highlighting how different elements relate through group operations.
