Visualization of Algebraic Structures - Symmetric Groups

Welcome! This project provides interactive visualizations of Cayley graphs of symmetric groups. Each visualization explores different generator sets and subgroup structures. Hover on the left edge or move your mouse over the sidebar area to reveal the menu. Click an item to explore the corresponding Cayley graph.

We focus on the symmetric group \(S_n\), the group of all permutations on \(n\) elements. Each Cayley graph visually represents the group structure, highlighting how elements relate under the chosen generators.
